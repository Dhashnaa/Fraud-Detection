Challenges and Scope in Credit Card Fraud Detection

Credit cards have become a fundamental part of modern commerce, facilitating seamless transactions both online and offline.Several techniques have been employed for credit card fraud detection, ranging from rule-based systems to advanced machine learning and deep learning models. Early fraud detection systems relied heavily on rule- based techniques. These systems used predefined rules to flag anomalous transactions. However, this convenience comes with a significant risk—fraudulent transactions.The proposed system is designed to be scalable, efficient, and adaptable, achieving superior performance metrics such as accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score compared to traditional methods.
